ABILITIES

ARCANE MASTERY (PASSIVE):

Ryze gains 5% of bonus mana per 100 of Ability Power.

OVERLOAD (Q):

Ryze shots an orb of pure energy that collides with the first enemy unit. If other enemies are marked with flux, overload spread to those units as well.

RUNE PRISION (W):

Ryze cast a rune prision that slow down an enemy. If the enemy is marked with flux it get rooted instead.

SPELL FLUX (E):

Ryze throw an energy orb to an enemy that gets marked with flux. Nearby enemies get marked with flux as well. Other abilities interacts with enemies marked with flux.

REALM WARP (R):

Ryze teleports himself and close ally units to a near place. Ryze's Overload that hits enemies marked with flux deals (40%/70%/100%) extra damage.

EARLY GAME RYZE HERE

EARLY GAME

In the early stage of the game Ryze is a weak and ambushable champion, it has low damage and no escape at all pre level 6. Because of that, is important that you focus on farm and avoid fights until you build Lost Chapter (it gives Ryze a large amount of mana and mana sustainability). At the beginning of the match you can go to Tear of the Goddes, if you want to accelerate the Seraph's Embrace build, or Doran's Ring if you want a more standard and mana sustainable early game.

MID GAME RYZE HERE

MID GAME

Post level 6 you can start trading more agressively and more often, due to the passive of Realm Warp. But, if you are playing in the middle lane you now have a strong weapon of gank, Realm Warp povides you a teleport that can be used to roam more efficiently and to gank powerfully (you can take the jungler with you!), if you have one or two itens in your build you have a somewhat strong damage. Remember that in level 2 and 3 of Realm Warp the distance of the teleport raises significantly.

LATE GAME RYZE HERE

LATE GAME

In the late game is where Ryze's potential is fully unleashed (most of the time), a Overload combined with Spell Flux can destroy an enemy team if well casted. But, if you can't find the space to land a devastating machine-gun of spells you can wait for the enemy team to enter in your composition and, then, land some Spell Flux + Overload combos. It's good for you to know that Ryze has a huge advantage in fights for greater objectives (i'm talking Baron and Dragons), that is even more true if the enemy team is inside the pit.
